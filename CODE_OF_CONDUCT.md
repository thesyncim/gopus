# Code of Conduct

`gopus` is a respectful, technically rigorous project. Keep feedback focused on
code, behavior, and evidence; welcome contributors of every background; and
accept correction gracefully.

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, trolling, publishing someone's
private information, and unwelcome sexual attention are not tolerated in any
project space — issues, pull requests, and reviews included.

Report problems to `thesyncim@gmail.com`. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, close threads, reject contributions, or block participants who violate
this code; severe or repeated violations lead to longer restrictions.
